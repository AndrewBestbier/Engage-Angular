What is the photoelectric effect? What is centripetal force? Where is the femur? What are noble gases? What are economies of scale? Where was Nelson Mandela born?

Engage allows students to ask questions anonymously and in real time. Engage your students like never before, for free!

Availiable on any platform

Engage can be accessed through any smart device: iPhone, Android, Laptops and Tablets. Students can simply use the devices they use everyday.

Truely Engaging

Engage has been tried and tested in real classrooms. Bring your students back into class by giving them another channel to participate through.

Free

Engage is developed by students for students. Help us improve Engage by emailing your suggestions. Let's improve the educational system together.
